CHANGING YOUTUBE VIDEOS:

Thumbnails:
If the youtube link is: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VIDEO_ID 
-> you will use https://img.youtube.com/vi/VIDEO_ID/default.jpg, but REPLACE the end "default" of "default.jpg" with either: "mqdefault" or "hqdefault". Make sure you keep the '.jpg' at the end.
